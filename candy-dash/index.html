Candy Dash

"Statistically, most of you will hit the first obstacle."

Space or ⤒ to jump — press again mid-air to double jump if you have 2+ candy charged. Arrow keys, or slide your thumb on the ◀ ▶ track, to explore (nothing auto-scrolls); hold Down/S, or pull your thumb down on the track, to crouch and aim low. Enter or ⏎ to talk, pull levers, and interact with whatever's in front of you. Collect Matrix-energy candies to charge Sparkles' horn (3 per shot, and it also trickles back slowly on its own — wait around if you run dry); press X or ✦ to fire a bolt and purify a corrupted creature back to its true form. Every level hides a piece of an ancient rune on a high platform — find it before the hollow tree at the end will let you through. Assemble the Rune of Resilience at the pedestal to earn an energy shield, then face each realm's Saurosapien boss — beating him gives Sparkles the energy to beam the portal open. Two realms await: the Whispering Forest, then the Sun-Blasted Dunes, where the sand itself gives way beneath your feet — and healed critters now stay to keep you company. Careful, though: shooting a critter that's already been redeemed hurts it for real and it's gone for good, so purify each one just once. Beat both realms and you can add your name (and your redeemed/lost/time stats) to the leaderboard.
