Candy Dash

"Statistically, most of you will hit the first obstacle."

Space or ⤒ to jump — press again mid-air to double jump if you have 2+ candy charged. Arrow keys, or slide your thumb on the ◀ ▶ track, to explore (nothing auto-scrolls); hold Down/S or ▼ to crouch and aim low. Collect Matrix-energy candies to charge Sparkles' horn (3 per shot, and it also trickles back slowly on its own — wait around if you run dry); press X or ✦ to fire a bolt and purify a corrupted creature back to its true form. Every level hides a piece of an ancient rune on a high platform — find it before the hollow tree at the end will let you through. The last level holds a mystical pedestal: slide the five pieces back into the Rune of Resilience to earn an energy shield, then beware the Forest Captain, who hunts you down — beating him gives Sparkles the energy to beam the portal open.
